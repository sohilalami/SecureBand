Agenda

The Avengers is an American action film from 2012, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Pictures. The story is based on the Marvel Comics with the same name. It is the sixth part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, after Iron Man, The Incredible Hulk, Iron Man 2, Thor and Captain America: The First Avenger.

Avengers: Infinity War is an American action science fiction film from Marvel Studios from 2018, directed by Anthony and Joe Russo. It is the nineteenth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the third Avengers film. The film revolves around the battle between the Avengers and the intergalactic super villain Thanos.

Thanos is a character from the comics of Marvel Comics. He was conceived by Jim Starlin and made his debut in Iron Man # 55 (February 1973). His name is derived from Thanatos, the personification of death in Greek Mythology. The character has also appeared afterwards in animation series, video and the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
